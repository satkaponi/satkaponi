Seller Onboarding Platform

Key highlights

  • Business category: Online marketplace for goods
  • Presence: Globally, more than 160 countries
  • Content type: Interactive learning
  • Languages: 2 - English, Hindi

Interactive content to enable easy onboarding of sellers

An online marketplace, based in USA, has a large number of sellers who list their products on the platform, which are to be purchased by customers across the world. The corporation wants to provide easy mechanism through which sellers can quickly learn onboarding, and listing of products. A series of interactive videos have been developed, which explain the entire process of onboarding easily. Videos are provided in multiple languages, in order to reach out to larger number of sellers.
